SibylFS

SibylFS is a filesystem test oracle which can take traces of filesystem behaviour (observed at the libc interface) and determine whether they conform to POSIX (or Linux, or Mac OS X) behaviour. The oracle is based on a specification which is in some sense a formal, mechanized version of (parts of) the POSIX specification.
